Smart Lighting System

INTRODUCTION

The Smart Lighting System Web Application is a project by deol satish, a student of Bachelor of Software Engineering (Honors) in his third year

Instructions for users:-

  • Users can create and login to accounts using the registration and login pages.
  • Registered users can then register their devices and see a list of the devices they have under their name.
  • By clicking on a device in the device list page, the light data for the device can be viewed.
  • You can the toggle the lights on/off.
